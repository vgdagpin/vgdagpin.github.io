Privacy Policy for TeeLedger

Effective Date: March 30, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 30, 2026

1. Introduction

TeeLedger ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal finance and ledger application developed to help you track your income, expenses, bills, and transactions. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using TeeLedger, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Financial Data You Enter

All financial data is created by you and stored locally on your device:

  • Ledgers, accounts, and account numbers
  • Transactions (income, expenses, transfers)
  • Bills, billers, and payment history
  • Budget periods and collections
  • Spending categories

2.2 Receipt Images

When you use the receipt scanning feature, receipt images you capture or select are:

  • Stored locally on your device
  • Uploaded to our server solely for OCR (optical character recognition) processing
  • Used to extract transaction details (merchant, amount, date, items)

2.3 Authentication Data

We store the following credentials securely in your device's native secure storage (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain):

  • A user ID and a locally generated authentication certificate (JWT secret)
  • An optional 4-digit PIN
  • A flag indicating whether biometric unlock is enabled

We never store your raw PIN or biometric data outside of the platform-provided secure storage. Authentication tokens are generated on your device and only the signed JWT is sent to our server — your secret never leaves the device.

2.4 Device Capabilities Used

  • Biometric authentication (fingerprint / Face ID) — to unlock the app locally
  • File system access — to read/write the local SQLite database
  • Image picker / document picker — to select receipt images from your device
  • Network access — to sync data to our server and process receipts

We do not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or calendar.

3. How We Use Your Information

Purpose Data Used
Display your financial records Financial data stored locally
Cloud sync (backup & multi-device access) Financial data sent to our server
Receipt OCR processing Receipt images sent to our server
App security & unlock PIN and biometric preference stored locally
API authentication Signed JWT token (no secrets transmitted)

4. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All your financial data is stored in an offline-first local SQLite database on your device. The app is fully functional without an internet connection.

Cloud Sync

If you use the cloud sync feature, your financial data (transactions, accounts, bills, etc.) is synced to our servers over HTTPS. Data is transmitted using authenticated, encrypted connections (JWT Bearer tokens over TLS).

Receipt Processing

Receipt images are uploaded over HTTPS to our server for OCR analysis and are used only to extract transaction details.

Security Practices

  • Authentication credentials are stored in platform-native secure storage (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain)
  • JWT tokens are signed on-device using HMAC-SHA256
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or third-party tracking SDKs are embedded in the app

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Your data may only be transmitted to:

  • Our own servers (api.teeledger.com) — for cloud sync and receipt OCR processing

6. Data Retention

  • Local data remains on your device until you uninstall the app or manually clear app data
  • Cloud-synced data is retained on our servers for as long as your account is active
  • You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data stored about you
  • Delete your account and all associated data from our servers
  • Export your data (available through the app's sync/export features)
  • Opt out of cloud sync — the app works fully offline without syncing

8. Children's Privacy

TeeLedger is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
